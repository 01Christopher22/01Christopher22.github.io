North Deck at Mom's House

My sister and I removed the the old deck on the north side of my moms house that had been there for 25+ years and both re-designed and constructed a new one. A fair amount of prep work had to be done. There was a large (36-in diameter) "dwarf" cottonwood tree that was growing too close to the house as well as a large ash tree that had to be removed. This work was performed mostly by tree removal professionals. Most of the old deck was deconstructed by my sister before I flew in. I led the design and construction of the new deck but my sister was an incredible help through the whole process and was the only reason why we were able build the new deck in a week. While construction has been completed there is a fair amount of landscape work that remains.
